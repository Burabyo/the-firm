Integrity. Dedication. Justice.

Who We Are

King & Co. Advocates is a full-service law firm based in Kigali, Rwanda. With a team of dedicated professionals, we deliver legal solutions grounded in experience, innovation, and strategic thinking.

Our Mission

To provide excellent and reliable legal services that uphold justice and empower clients to thrive.

Our Vision

To be a benchmark law firm in Africa, known for shaping legal discourse and achieving precedent-setting results.

Our Values

  • Integrity
  • Confidentiality
  • Client-Centeredness
  • Excellence

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Senior Advocate Jean Abayo, our firm has grown into a dynamic team with experience in litigation, arbitration, corporate law, and human rights. We are proud of our legacy of trust and our forward-looking approach.

We are a diverse, multilingual, and professional team committed to upholding justice and empowering communities.
